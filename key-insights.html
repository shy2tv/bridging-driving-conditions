Key Research Insights

Our paper contains several interesting findings that provide deeper understanding of domain adaptation for adverse driving conditions:

Auxiliary Inputs Matter

Depth and semantic maps significantly improve object preservation during domain adaptation. Different configurations have distinctive effects on the generated images.

Cross-Weather Transfer

Training on certain weather conditions provides unexpected benefits for others, suggesting shared underlying features across different adverse conditions.

Size Distribution Challenges

Clear weather scenes often contain smaller, more difficult objects - a counterintuitive finding that explains certain performance patterns.
